I am a tenure-track assistant professor with the School of Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen). I received Ph.D. and M.A.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Toronto in 2020 and 2013, respectively, and B.Eng. in Information Security and B.Sc. in Mathematics both from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2011. My recent research interests lie in the realization of intelligent reflecting surface in the real-world 5G network [Video]. My work on fractional programming was awarded the IEEE Signal Processing Society Young Author Best Paper. I serve as an Editor of IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications.

Research

  • Optimization for Machine Learning & Data Science

  • Information Theory
