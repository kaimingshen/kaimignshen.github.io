I am a tenure-track assistant professor with the School of Science and Engineering at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (Shenzhen).

I received Ph.D. and M.A.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Toronto in 2020 and 2013, respectively, and B.Eng. in Information Security and B.Sc. in Mathematics both from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2011.

Research

  • 5G/6G Wireless Networks (e.g., URLLC and Massive MIMO)

  • Machine Learning (esp. its applications to IoT)

  • Optimization for Data Analysis

  • Network Information Theory

I am looking for Ph.D./Master students and undergraduate students with solid mathematics background to work on machine learning, wireless communications, and optimization.
